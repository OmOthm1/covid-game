About Game

Welcome to the thrilling world of the "COVID Game"! This exciting browser-based game offers an endless challenge that keeps you engaged as you strive to achieve the highest score possible. With a never-ending difficulty curve, the adrenaline rush never stops!

Objective:

Your mission is to survive as long as possible, racking up points by eliminating waves of pesky viruses. As the difficulty increases, so does the excitement!

The Viruses:

  • Green Virus: The game starts with this basic virus. It moves slowly, inflicts damage on touch, and has moderate health. when two of them come into contact, they combine into a bigger virus.
  • Exploding Red Virus: As you progress, the faster and more dangerous Exploding Red Virus appears. When killed, it explodes, damaging nearby enemies, or you if you're too close. It has low health.
  • Big Purple Virus: Prepare for a challenge as the formidable Big Purple Virus emerges. This massive virus is slow but unleashes little circles that deal damage, and touching it can be hazardous. It boasts an impressive health pool that won't be easy to deplete.

Powerups:

Perform well, and you'll be rewarded! Defeating viruses in rapid succession has a chance to trigger powerups. Collect these to gain a temporary advantage:

  • Heart: Grants an extra life to extend your survival.
  • Face Mask: Temporarily grants invincibility, allowing you to maneuver freely amidst the viruses unharmed.
  • Boots: Provides a temporary speed boost, enabling you to dodge incoming threats with agility.
  • Potion: Enhances your strength, allowing you to deal more damage to viruses for a limited time.

Health Objects:

Take down viruses to reveal health objects. The stronger the virus, the bigger the health object it drops. Collect these to increase your health and stay in the game longer!

Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move your character or hold the Left Click to make the player follow your cursor.
  • Press the Space Bar for a standard attack, with the attack's size increasing the longer you hold the key.
  • Alternatively, use the Left Mouse Button for attack if you prefer a mouse-based control scheme.
  • Hit P to pause or unpause the game.
  • To mute or unmute the game, press the M key.
